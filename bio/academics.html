Bio

Theodore Lindsey is a Masters student in Computer Science at the University of Kansas. He studies data mining and is advised by Professor Jerzy Grzymala-Busse. In May 2014, he earned a Masters degree in Mathematics from the University of Kansas working with Professor Bozenna Pasik-Duncan. His interests are situated at the intersection of programming and mathematics. In particular, he is interested in data processing and mining and making sense of the data deluge.

Theodore has taught a number of undergraduate courses, including several levels of algebra and calculus. He also has extensive tutoring experience and has tutored mathematics professionally since before beginning his undergraduate studies.
